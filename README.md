# 🧠 About the Project

The **Mobile Sales & Customer Analytics Dashboard** provides a comprehensive view of both sales performance and customer behavior, enabling decision-makers to monitor key business KPIs and uncover actionable insights. It combines sales analytics with customer intelligence to track revenue, transactions, growth trends, top-performing products, and regional performance while also analyzing customer demographics, purchasing behavior, satisfaction, and loyalty.

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

### 📈 Sales Dashboard
- Monitor overall sales performance
- Track Year-over-Year (YoY) Growth
- Analyze monthly and weekly sales trends
- Identify top-selling mobile models
- Compare sales across different cities
- Understand customer payment preferences
- Evaluate average selling price and average order value

### 👥 Customer Dashboard
- Analyze customer demographics
- Identify the best-selling customer
- Discover the top 10 customers by sales
- Evaluate customer satisfaction through ratings
- Analyze customer distribution by age group
- Compare customer distribution across mobile brands
- Track sales and units sold for individual customers
